Vegetable Stir Fry

There are many vegetables which add a great crunch to this stir fry.

Ingridients:

  • Stir Fry Noodles
  • Brocoli Heads
  • Baby Corn
  • Cashews
  • 2 tbs canola oil

Steps:

  1. Steam all vegitables for 20 minutes, covered
  2. Boil the noodles for 10 minutes
  3. Fry all ingridients tother in wok with canola oil ofr 5 minutes, stir frequently
