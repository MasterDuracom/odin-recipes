Salmon Burger

This is reminescent of a salmon burger that was made at burger lounge in Hillcrest San Diego until a few years ago. Shame they got rid of it but the recipe is very simple, they salmon is just hard to grind.

Ingridients:

  • Ground Salmon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fresh basil
  • 1 tps sea salt
  • 1 tsp virgin olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oil in skillet on high heat
  2. Mix all ingridients together
  3. Flatten into patty shape
  4. Grill on skillet for 3 minutes each side or until sear is realized
