Fried Egg

Here is a fried egg. First thing I ever learned to cook. Courtesy of my step dad from Peru. .

Ingridients:

  • Egg
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Steps:

  1. heat oil in small pan
  2. crack egg in pan
  3. flip after 1 minute on high, add salt and pepper to taste
